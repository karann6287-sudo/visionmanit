About Us

Mission


To ignite scientific curiosity and foster active learning in Arduino, hardware , and embedded system by promoting student-led research, encouraging interdisciplinary collaboration, and organizing impactful events that bridge theoretical knowledge with practical application.


Vision


To become a leading student-driven platform that promotes embedded system, promotes Arduino, and bridges the gap between theory and space practical projects in India.
